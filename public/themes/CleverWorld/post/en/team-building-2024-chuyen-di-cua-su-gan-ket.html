The core of a strong team lies in connection, where each person feels like an essential part and plays a crucial role in the collective success of the group.   

With that spirit, Clever World has made its annual team-building event an important activity and a distinctive cultural feature, attracting the attention of all members.

Held in the coastal city of Mũi Né from August 2nd to 4th, Clever World’s 2024 Team Building event brought together all members from various departments under the theme “Work with passion - Celebrate with spirit”     

During this trip, the members had the chance to discover the many hidden surprises of Mũi Né, a land “seemingly familiar yet full of surprises”, from the rustic coastal dishes to the breathtaking natural landscapes of the seaside city.    



In addition, through lively and youthful team games, every member had the opportunity to bond with colleagues from different departments, further enhancing the solidarity within Clever World's family.
